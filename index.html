Mushfiqa Ayesha

Building small, considered games — and writing about the systems underneath them.

I'm an independent game developer publishing on the Play Store, currently building Merge and Serve, a cozy merge-and-automation cooking game, alongside an earlier creature-collection title. I work mostly in Unity and TypeScript, with a focus on systems that stay legible even as they grow — crafting chains, automation loops, and the small feedback systems that make a game feel alive.

Outside of game dev, I write about systems thinking, cognition, and complexity on Substack, and I'm exploring graduate research in brain-computer interfaces — looking at how behavioral signals from interactive systems can serve as proxies for cognitive states.
