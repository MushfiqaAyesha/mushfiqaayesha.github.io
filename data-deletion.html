Data Deletion & Ad Personalization - Sticker Station Idle

Sticker Station Idle does not store personal data directly. The app uses Google AdMob to show general-audience advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and ad interaction data for advertising purposes.

How Users Can Delete or Manage Their Data

You can control or delete the data collected by AdMob using the following methods:

  • Reset or opt out of your device's Advertising ID: Go to your device Settings → Google → Ads → "Opt out of Ads Personalization".
  • Manage ad personalization in your Google account: Visit https://adssettings.google.com to review and adjust your ad personalization settings or delete ad-related data.

No other personal data is collected or retained by Sticker Station Idle.

This page serves as the official instructions for users of Sticker Station Idle to manage or delete any data collected via AdMob advertising.
