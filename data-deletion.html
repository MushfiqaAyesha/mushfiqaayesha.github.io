Data Deletion & Ad Personalization

Our mobile applications do not store personal data (such as names or emails) directly on our servers. The Apps use Google AdMob to show advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and ad interaction data for advertising and analytics purposes.

How to Manage or Delete Your Data

You can control, reset, or delete the data collected by our third-party advertising partners across all our Apps using the following methods:

  • In-App Privacy Settings: Open the settings menu in any of our Apps and click "Privacy Settings" (where available) to re-open the consent form and change your preferences.
  • Reset Advertising ID (Android): Go to your device Settings → Google → Ads → "Reset Advertising ID" or "Delete Advertising ID".
  • Reset IDFA (iOS): Go to Settings → Privacy → Tracking.
  • Google Account Settings: Visit adssettings.google.com to adjust your global Google ad settings.

No other personal data is collected, stored, or retained by Mushfiqa.

This page provides universal instructions for users of all mobile applications published by Mushfiqa to manage or delete any data collected via third-party services.
