Merge and Serve

A cozy merge-and-cook game where you combine ingredients and tools to discover recipes, automate your kitchen with hoppers and auto-ejects, and serve a growing menu of dishes across a tiered progression of stages.

  • Automation systems: hoppers, auto-eject, speed upgrades
  • Achievement and dish-mastery progression
  • Built with React, TypeScript, and Capacitor for Android
